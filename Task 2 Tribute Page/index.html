About


Sundar Pichai is the Indian-American Business Executive and one of the most important personalities in the world. Since 10 August 2015, he became very famous because on this date, he was announced to be the new CEO of Google. Formerly, he was the Product Chief of Google.
Sundar Pichai Born on July 12, 1972, in Madurai, India, Pichai, from a very early age started taking interest in technology-related knowledge. He started his early education at Jawahar Vidyalaya. After completing his secondary education, Pichai got enrolled in Vana Vani School to complete his intermediate-level studies. Sundar Pichai then got a degree in metallurgical engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. Pichai also holds M.S. and MBA degrees from Stanford University and Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania respectively.
